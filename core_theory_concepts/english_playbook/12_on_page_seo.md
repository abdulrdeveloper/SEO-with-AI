# Topic 12: On-Page SEO & How to Do It Using AI

## 1. What is On-Page SEO?
On-Page SEO (also known as on-site SEO) is the practice of optimizing the elements *on* your website to rank higher on search engines. While Off-Page SEO is about backlinks (things outside your site), On-Page SEO is entirely under your control.

## 2. Key Elements of On-Page SEO
To perfectly optimize a webpage, you need to fix the following elements:

1.  **Title Tag (Meta Title):** The clickable blue title in search results. Must be catchy, include the main keyword, and be under 60 characters.
    * **If you don't do this:** Google will show its own generated title, which is often irrelevant and boring.
2.  **Meta Description:** The short summary below the title. Must include variations of the keyword, a call-to-action (CTA), and be under 160 characters.
    * **If you don't do this:** Lower click-through rates, and Google will just pull random text from the page.
3.  **URL (Slug):** The web address of the page. It should be short, readable, and include the primary keyword (e.g., `website.com/on-page-seo` instead of `website.com/page?id=123`).
    * **If you don't do this:** The URL looks spammy to users and confusing for search engines.
4.  **Heading Tags (H1, H2, H3):** Proper structure. Only ONE `H1` tag per page (the main title). Use `H2` for main sub-topics, `H3` for points under them.
    * **If you don't do this:** The content becomes a wall of text, frustrating both readers and Google bots.
5.  **Keyword Optimization:** Placing your primary keyword in the first 100 words, using LSI (related) keywords naturally, and avoiding "keyword stuffing" (repeating the keyword too many times unnaturally).
    * **If you don't do this:** Search engines will struggle to identify the primary topic of your page.
6.  **Image Optimization:** Search engines can't "see" images. You must give images SEO-friendly file names (e.g., `blue-nike-shoes.jpg`) and add **Alt Text** describing the image.
    * **If you don't do this:** You lose out completely on potential traffic from Google Images search.
7.  **Internal & External Links:** Linking to other pages *within* your site (Internal) and linking out to authoritative, trustworthy sites (External).
    * **If you don't do this:** The pages act as dead ends, preventing the flow of authority.
8.  **Content Length:** Google tends to favor detailed, long-form content.
    * **Top Tip:** Write slightly longer content than your top-ranking competitors — if they wrote 800 words, you write 1200.

## 3. Doing On-Page SEO using AI (The Smart Way)
Doing all this manually takes hours — with AI, it takes minutes. We will use advanced AI like Claude, ChatGPT (GPT-4o), or Gemini to do the heavy lifting for us and ensure perfect optimization.

### AI Prompt for On-Page SEO
Copy and paste this prompt into your AI tool. Just replace the text in the brackets `[ ]` with your own information.

```text
I am writing a new webpage/blog post. 
Here is my Primary Keyword: [INSERT MAIN KEYWORD]
Here are my Secondary/LSI Keywords: [INSERT 3-4 RELATED KEYWORDS]
Here is the rough draft, topic, or main points of my content: [INSERT YOUR ROUGH TEXT OR TOPIC IDEA HERE]

Return each section with a clear label. Keep the tone human, not robotic.

Please generate a complete, highly optimized On-Page SEO strategy and content for this page. Provide the output in the following structure:

1. URL Slug: Provide a short, SEO-friendly URL slug.
2. Meta Title: Write a catchy, click-worthy Meta Title (maximum 60 characters) that includes the Primary Keyword.
3. Meta Description: Write an engaging Meta Description (maximum 160 characters) with a clear Call to Action (CTA), including the primary keyword.
4. Heading Structure: Provide an outline using proper H1, H2, and H3 tags. The H1 must be highly compelling.
5. Optimized Content: Write the full body content based on my draft/topic. 
    - Naturally integrate the Primary and LSI keywords. 
    - Ensure it is highly readable with short paragraphs and bullet points.
    - Write in an engaging, human tone that satisfies search intent.
6. Image Alt Text Suggestions: Provide 2-3 ideas for images I should include, along with proper SEO-optimized exact Alt Text for each.
```
