# Types of SEO (The 3 Main Pillars)

To rank a website on Google, you have to work on three main areas. These are known as the three pillars or types of SEO.

## 1. On-Page SEO (Inside your website)
This is everything you do **directly on your website's pages** to help them rank higher. You have full control over this.
*   **Keywords:** Using the words people are searching for in your content.
*   **High-Quality Content:** Writing helpful, clear, and valuable articles.
*   **Titles & Headings:** Using proper titles (H1) and subheadings (H2, H3).
*   **Images:** Adding alt text so search engines know what the image is about.

## 2. Off-Page SEO (Outside your website)
This is everything you do **outside of your website** to show Google that your site is popular and trustworthy.
*   **Backlinks:** Getting other popular websites to link to your site (This is like getting "votes" on the internet).
*   **Social Media:** Sharing your links on platforms like Twitter, LinkedIn, or Facebook to get more traffic.
*   **Brand Mentions:** When people talk about your brand on other blogs or forums.

## 3. Technical SEO (Behind the scenes)
This has nothing to do with the actual text or content. It focuses on the **technical structure** of your website so search engine bots can easily crawl and index it.
*   **Website Speed:** Making sure your site loads very fast.
*   **Mobile-Friendly:** Ensuring the website looks good and works perfectly on mobile phones.
*   **XML Sitemaps:** Providing a map of your website so bots can find all your pages easily.
*   **Security:** Using HTTPS (SSL Certificate) to keep user data secure.
