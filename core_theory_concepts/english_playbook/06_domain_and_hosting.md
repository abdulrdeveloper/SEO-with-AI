# Buying Quality Domain & Web Hosting

---

## 1. What is a Domain Name?
Your **Domain Name** is your website's address on the internet. It's what people type into the browser to find you.
> **Example:** `google.com`, `learnseowithai.com`
> **Analogy:** If your website is a house, the domain name is the **home address**.

---

## 2. What is Web Hosting?
**Web Hosting** is the physical server space where all your website's files, images, and code are stored. 
> **Analogy:** If your website is a house, hosting is the **plot of land** it is built on. 

---

## 3. Why Does Quality Hosting Matter for SEO?
Buying pure, high-quality hosting isn't just about keeping your website live—it is a **direct ranking factor for SEO**.

| SEO Factor | Why it Matters | Rule of Thumb |
|------------|----------------|---------------|
| **Server Speed** | Slow servers lead to high bounce rates. | TTFB should be < 200ms. |
| **Uptime** | If Google tries to crawl your site and it's down, it drops your rank. | Look for **99.9% Uptime** guarantees. |
| **Security** | Good hosts provide free SSL (HTTPS) and malware protection. | Never buy hosting without free SSL. |
| **Server Location**| If your audience is in India, a server in India loads faster than one in the USA. | Choose a server close to your audience. |
