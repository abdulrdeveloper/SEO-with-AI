# Topic 17: E-E-A-T Aur Google Quality Guidelines

## 1. E-E-A-T Kya Hai?
E-E-A-T Google ke ranking system ka ek bohot bada hissa hai, khaas tor par aaj kal ke SEO mein. Iska matlab hai:
*   **E - Experience (Tajarba):** Kya likhne wale ne khud us cheez ka tajarba kiya hai? (Jaise product khud use kar ke review dena).
*   **E - Expertise (Maharat):** Kya writer us field ka maahir (expert) hai?
*   **A - Authoritativeness (Haisiyat):** Kya us industry mein log aur doosri websites aapko ek authority mantay hain?
*   **T - Trustworthiness (Bharosa):** Sabase zaroori cheez! Kya website safe hai? Kya ispar contact info aur About page sahi se diya gaya hai?

## 2. YMYL Topics Kya Hote Hain?
YMYL ka matlab hai **"Your Money or Your Life"** (Aapka Paisa ya Aapki Zindagi).
Aise topics jin par parh kar kisi ki health, wealth (paise), ya safety par asar padh sakta ho (Jaise Medical advice, Finance, Legal law). Google in topics par aam logon ki sites rank nahi karta. Agar aap koi Health blog chalate hain lekin aap doctor nahi hain, toh E-E-A-T ki kami ki wajah se rank karna lagbhag namumkin hai.

## 3. Apni Site Ka E-E-A-T Kaise Behtar Karein?
1.  **Author Bios (Likhne Wale Ki Detail):** Har article ke neeche likhne wale ki picture, uski degree, aur LinkedIn ka link hona chahiye.
2.  **About Us Page:** Aapki website kiski hai, owner kon hai, aur aapka maqsad kya hai yeh properly likha hona chahiye.
3.  **Badi Sites Ka Hawala (Citations):** Jab koi fact batayen, toh kisi badi aur sachchi website (jaise .gov ya .edu) ka reference link zaroor dein.
4.  **Content Update Rakhein:** 3 saal purani information ko update karte rahein taake user ko hamesha sahi knowledge mile.

## 4. AI Se E-E-A-T Build Karna
E-E-A-T prove karne ke liye ek strong, professional aur credible Author Bio ka hona bohot zaroori hai. Hum AI ka use kar ke exact Google ki requirement ke hisaab se apna bio likhwa sakte hain.

```text
- Name: [APNA NAAM LIKHEIN]

```
