# Topic 10: Keyword Research Ke Best Tools (Manual Research)

---

## Keyword Research Tools Ki Zaroorat Kyun Hoti Hai?

Jab hum manual keyword research karte hain, toh humein andaza lagane ki bajaye data chahiye hota hai. Yeh tools humein batate hain:
- **Search Volume:** Ek mahine mein kitne log yeh word search karte hain.
- **Keyword Difficulty (KD):** Is keyword par rank karna kitna aasaan ya mushkil hai.
- **Related Keywords:** Naye keywords aur long-tail ideas jo hamare zehen mein nahi aaye.

Neeche kuch behtareen tools (Free aur Paid) diye gaye hain jo manual keyword research mein use hote hain:

---

## 1. Google Ads Keyword Planner (Free)
Yeh Google ka apna official tool hai. Waise toh yeh ads chalane walon ke liye bana hai, lekin SEO mein keyword ideas lene ke liye yeh sabse zyada use hota hai.
- **Fayda:** Data direct Google ki taraf se aata hai, isliye bohot authentic hota hai.
- **Kis liye best hai:** Naye primary keywords dhoondne aur search volume ka andaza lagane ke liye.
- **Kaise use karein:** Google Ads par free account banayein, "Discover new keywords" mein apna topic likhein, aur tool aapko hazaron ideas de dega.

## 2. Google Trends (Free)
Yeh tool aapko exact search volume toh nahi batata, lekin yeh batata hai ke **kisi keyword ka trend kaisa chal raha hai**.
- **Fayda:** Is se pata chalta hai ke keyword waqt ke sath popular ho raha hai, down jaa raha hai, ya koi seasonal (khas mausam/tyohar par search hone wala) word hai.
- **Kis liye best hai:** 2 ya 3 keywords ko aapas mein compare karne ke liye ke aaj kal konsa zyada chal raha hai.

## 3. Mangools (KWFinder) (Freemium/Paid)
Mangools ka KWFinder beginners ke liye sabse behtareen aur asaan tool maana jata hai.
- **Fayda:** Iska user-interface kamal ka hai aur iska "Keyword Difficulty" score sabse accurate hota hai.
- **Kis liye best hai:** Aise **long-tail keywords** nikaalne ke liye jin par competition bohot kam ho aur nayi website asani se rank kar sake. Ismein green color ka matlab hota hai ke rank karna asaan hai.

## 4. WordStream Free Keyword Tool
WordStream ek zabardast free tool deta hai jo bohat saare paid tools ko takkar deta hai.
- **Fayda:** Yeh fast hai aur bina account banaye aapko ache keyword suggestions, unka search volume, aur competition level bata deta hai.
- **Kis liye best hai:** Apni specific industry (maslan Real Estate, Tech) aur location ke hisaab se keywords dhoondne ke liye.

## 5. KeywordTool.io & New Keyword Finders (Freemium)
Jab aap Google ke search box mein kuch type karte hain, toh Google jo neeche suggestions (autocomplete) deta hai, yeh tool un sabko nikaal lata hai.
- **Fayda:** Google Keyword planner aksar kuch lambe (long-tail) keywords chupa leta hai, yeh tool unhe dhoond nikalta hai.
- **Kis liye best hai:** Question-based keywords (Jaise: How to, What is) dhoondne ke liye. Yeh sirf Google nahi, balke **YouTube, Amazon, aur App Store** ke keywords bhi nikaal kar deta hai.

---

## Manual Keyword Research Karne Ka Asaan Tareeqa (Strategy):
1. Apna thora broad main topic **WordStream** ya **Keyword Tool** mein dalein taaki zyada se zyada ideas mil sakein.
2. Un ideas ko **Google Keyword Planner** mein dalein taaki unka asal search volume pata chal sake.
3. Phir **Google Trends** par check karein ke uski popularity neechay toh nahi ja rahi.
4. Aakhir mein, final keywords ko **Mangools (KWFinder)** mein daal kar check karein ke difficulty score kam (Green) ho, taaki aap asani se rank kar sakein!
