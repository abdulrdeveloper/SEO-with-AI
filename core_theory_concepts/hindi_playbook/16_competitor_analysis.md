# Topic 16: SEO Competitor Analysis (Competitors Ka Traffic Churana)

## 1. SEO Competitor Analysis Kya Hai?
Competitor analysis ka matlab hai un websites ki jasoosi karna jo Google par aapse upar rank kar rahi hain. Tuke lagane (guessing) ke bajaye, hum un top 3 sites ka formula copy karte hain jise Google already pasand kar raha hai.

## 2. Humain Kya Dhoondna Hai?
Competitor ki website check karte waqt 3 cheezein sabse zaroori hain:
1.  **Keyword Gaps:** Wo kon se keywords hain jin par competitor ko traffic mil raha hai lekin aapne un par content nahi likha.
2.  **Backlinks (Links kahan se liye?):** Unko kin badi websites ne link diya hai? Agar koi site aapke competitor ko link de sakti hai, toh wo aapko bhi link de sakti hai.
3.  **Content Structure:** Unhone kitna lamba article likha hai? Konsi H2/H3 headings use ki hain? Kya unhone images aur videos lagaye hain?

## 3. Competitor Analysis Ke Best Tools
*   **Ahrefs aur SEMrush (Paid):** Yeh industry leadeers hain. Bas competitor ka URL dalein aur yeh tool aapko bata dega ke unhe kahan se traffic aa raha hai, unke best pages kon se hain, aur unke saare backlinks ki list de dega.
*   **Moz (Freemium):** Websites ki Domain Authority (DA) compare karne ke liye best hai.
*   **Ubersuggest (Free basic ver):** Beginners ke liye competitor ki basic traffic aur keywords nikaalne ke liye acha hai.

## 4. AI Se Content Gap Analysis Karna
Agar aapke paas paid tools nahi hain, toh AI aapka bohot time bacha sakta hai. Aap AI se apni post aur rank karne wali post ka muqabla karwa sakte hain taake pata chale ke aapne kya miss kiya.

### 🤖 Content Gap AI Prompt
Rank #1 wali site ka text copy karein, aur apna text copy karein. Dono ko is prompt ke sath ChatGPT ya Claude mein dalein:

```text
Act as a Senior SEO Strategist. 

I am trying to rank for the keyword: "[YAHAN MAIN KEYWORD LIKHEIN]".
I have provided two articles below. The first is the current #1 ranking competitor's article. The second is my draft article.

Competitor's Article:
[COMPETITOR KA TEXT YAHAN PASTE KAREIN]

My Article:
[APNA TEXT YAHAN PASTE KAREIN]

Please analyze both articles and provide a "Content Gap Analysis":
1. What specific sub-topics, entities, and questions did the competitor cover that I missed?
2. How can I improve my heading structure compared to theirs?
3. Provide 3-5 actionable recommendations on what paragraphs or sections I should add to make my article 10x better and more comprehensive than the competitor's.
```
**Yeh Kaise Madad Karega:** AI aapko bata dega ke competitor ne konsi aisi baat likhi thi jo aap bhool gaye. Issey aapka article hamesha #1 result wale se level-up (behtar aur detailed) ban jayega.
