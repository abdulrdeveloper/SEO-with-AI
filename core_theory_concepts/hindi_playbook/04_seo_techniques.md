# SEO Techniques: White, Black, aur Grey Hat

SEO karne ke alag-alag tareeqe hote hain. Google jaise search engines ke kuch apne rules hote hain. Aap in rules ko follow karte hain ya todte hain, is buniyad (base) par SEO ko 3 types mein divide kiya gaya hai:

## 1. White Hat SEO (Sahi Tareeqa)
Yeh SEO karne ka sabse safe aur ethical tareeqa hai. Isme hum Google ki saari guidelines ko strictly follow karte hain.
*   **Kaise karte hain?:** Isme hamara main focus bots par nahi, balki human users ke liye asli aur acha content banane par hota hai. Site ko fast banate hain aur natural tareeqe se dusri websites se respect (backlinks) earn karte hain.
*   **Results:** Isme result aane mein mehnat aur time lagta hai, par ek baar website rank ho jaye toh lambe samay tak rank karti rehti hai. Isme Google se ban (block) hone ka zero risk hota hai.

## 2. Black Hat SEO (Galat Tareeqa)
Yeh ek risky aur unethical tareeqa hai. Isme log short-cuts dhundhte hain aur Google ke algorithm ko cheat/trick karke rank karne ki koshish karte hain.
*   **Kaise karte hain?:** "Keyword stuffing" karna (ek hi keyword ko zabardasti 100 baar likhna), paise dekar fake links khareedna, ya page par text ka color background jaisa karke hidden text chupa dena.
*   **Results:** Shayad in tricks se website jaldi rank ho jaye, lekin Google AI bahut smart hai. Jab pakde jayenge, toh Google aapki website ko hamesha ke liye dande marega (Penalty) aur search results se puri tarah gayab kar dega.

## 3. Grey Hat SEO (Beech Ka Rasta)
Yeh White Hat aur Black Hat ke beech ka rasta hai.
*   **Kaise karte hain?:** Isme aap technically Google ke rules puri tarah se tod nahi rahe hote, par unhe thoda bahot manipulate zaroor kar rahe hote hain apney fayde ke liye ("bending the rules").
*   **Results:** Isme pure White hat ke comparison mein sayad jaldi result mil jaye, par risk hamesha apne sar par rehta hai. Kyun ki aaj ka Grey hat, kal aane wale kisi bhi naye Google update ke baad Black hat ban sakta hai!
