# Topic 18: Topic Clusters Aur Content Silos (Website Structure)

## 1. Topic Clusters Kya Hote Hain?
Pehle log apni website par random topics par alag alag posts likhte thay. Lekin aaj kal Google **Topical Authority** ko pasand karta hai (yaani Google ko yakeen dilana ke us ek khaas topic par aapse behtar koi nahi janta).

Topic Cluster (ya Content Silo) ka matlab hai apne content ko ek group (cluster) ki shakal mein structure karna. Iske 3 hisse hote hain:
1.  **Pillar Page:** Ek bohot lamba, detailed article (3000+ words) jo ek broad topic ko cover kare. (Jaise: "The Ultimate Guide to Digital Marketing").
2.  **Cluster Content:** Chotay aur specific articles jo usi topic se nikle hon. (Jaise: "SEO kya hai?", "Email marketing kaise karein").
3.  **Internal Linking:** Sabse zaroori hissa. Saare chotay (Cluster) articles ke andar main (Pillar) page ka link hona chahiye, aur Pillar page se saare cluster articles ka link hona chahiye. Ek jaal (web) ban jana chahiye.

## 2. Topic Clusters Kyun Kaam Karte Hain?
*   **Google Bots Ko Asani:** Jo web ya internal links aap banate hain, us se Google bots aapki website jaldi browse aur index kar lete hain.
*   **Link Juice Pass Hota Hai:** Agar aapke kisi ek chote article par acha link ya traffic aa gaya, toh internal links ki wajah se wo power aakhir mein aapke main Pillar Page tak pohanch jati hai aur uski ranking bhi badh jati hai.
*   **Expert Status:** Yeh technique Google ko bata deti hai ke inhon ne is topic ko completely cover kiya hai, isliye inhein top par rakhna chahiye.

## 3. AI Ke Zariye Topic Cluster Map Banana
Manually 20 se 30 related topics sochna aur unki list banana mushkil hota hai. AI is kaam ka maahir hai aur aapko mahinon ka content plan ek jhatke mein bana kar de sakta hai.

### 🤖 Topic Cluster Map Banane Ka AI Prompt
Is prompt ko use kar ke apni niche (category) ke liye SEO strategy taiyar karein:

```text
Act as a Master SEO Architect and Content Strategist.

I want to build Topical Authority for my website in the following niche/industry: "[YAHAN APNA NICHE LIKHEIN, jaise: Graphic Design, Weight Loss, ya Indoor Plants]".

My primary goal is to create a complete "Topic Cluster" strategy to dominate this niche.

Please generate a structured map including:
1. One Broad "Pillar Page" Title (Targeting a highly competitive short-tail keyword).
2. 10 highly specific "Cluster Page" Titles (Targeting easy-to-rank, long-tail and question-based keywords).
3. The Search Intent for each cluster page (Informational, Transactional, etc.).
4. A brief (1-sentence) description of how the internal linking will flow between these specific pages.

Present the results in a clean, easy-to-read table format.
```
**Fayda:** Is prompt se AI aapko ek proper plan bana kar dega. Phir aap blind ho kar content likhne is bajaye us table ke hisaab se systematic treeqe se articles likhna shuru kar sakte hain.
