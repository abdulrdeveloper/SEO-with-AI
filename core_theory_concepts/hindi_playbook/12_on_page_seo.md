# Topic 12: On-Page SEO Aur AI Se Ise Kaise Karein

## 1. On-Page SEO Kya Hai?
On-Page SEO (ya on-site SEO) ka matlab hai apni website ke **andar** maujood har cheez ko optimize/behtar karna taaki Google usay top par rank kare. (Off-Page SEO website ke bahar hota hai jaise links banana, lekin On-Page SEO 100% aapke control mein hota hai).

## 2. On-Page SEO Ke Main Elements Kya Hain?
Ek webpage ko perfect banane ke liye in cheezon par kaam kiya jata hai:

1.  **Title Tag (Meta Title):** Google search mein jo blue color ka clickable title nazar aata hai. Isme aapka main keyword hona chahiye aur yeh 60 characters se lamba nahi hona chahiye.
    * **Agar nahi kiya toh:** Google apni marzi ka title show karta hai — jo aksar bekar hota hai.
2.  **Meta Description:** Title ke neeche jo 2 lines ki description hoti hai. Isme user ko attract karne wali baat honi chahiye, primary keyword hona chahiye, aur yeh 160 characters se choti honi chahiye.
    * **Agar nahi kiya toh:** Log Google se aapki site par kam click karenge kyunke Google khud se page ka koi random text utha kar dikha dega.
3.  **URL (Slug):** Page ka link. Yeh chota aur saaf hona chahiye jisme keyword ho. (Misal: `site.com/seo-tips` theek hai, `site.com/page?id=123` galat hai).
    * **Agar nahi kiya toh:** Lamba aur ajeeb URL spammy lagta hai aur Google bhi confuse ho jata hai.
4.  **Heading Tags (H1, H2, H3):** Content ko sahi se format karna. Ek page par sirf EK `H1` (Main Heading) hona chahiye. Uske baad sub-headings ke liye `H2` aur phir `H3` ka use karein.
    * **Agar nahi kiya toh:** Pura content ek boring block ban jayega jise padhna mushkil hoga, aur Google bots ko samajh nahi aayega ke important topic kya hai.
5.  **Keyword Placement:** Apna main keyword content ke pehle 100 words mein daalna. Sath mein LSI (related) keywords use karna. (Lekin keyword stuffing na karein, yaani zabardasti baar baar keyword na thonsein).
    * **Agar nahi kiya toh:** Google ko pata hi nahi chalega ke aapka page kis topic ke baaray mein hai aur ranking gir jayegi.
6.  **Image Optimization:** Google pictures ko dekh nahi sakta, wo unhe padhta hai. Isliye image ka naam theek rakhein (jaise `blue-shoes.jpg`) aur image upload karte waqt uska **Alt Text** zaroor daalein (Jisme image describe ki gayi ho).
    * **Agar nahi kiya toh:** Google Images se jo free traffic milna tha, wo bilkul waste ho jayega.
7.  **Internal aur External Linking:** Apni website ke kisi doosre page ka link dena (Internal) aur kisi doosri badi website ka reference link dena (External).
    * **Agar nahi kiya toh:** Website ki authority aapas mein baant (flow) nahi payegi aur user ek hi page padh kar chala jayega.
8.  **Content Length:** Google detail aur long-form content ko zyada rank karta hai.
    * **Top Tip:** Apne competitors ke top ranking pages se thoda lamba content likho — agar woh 800 words mein hain toh tum 1200 likho.

## 3. AI Ka Use Karke On-Page SEO Karna (Smart Tareeqa)
Yeh sab manually karne mein ghante lagte hain — AI se minutes mein ho jaata hai. Hum Claude, ChatGPT, ya Gemini jaise advanced AI models ka use karenge apna time bachane aur best results nikaalne ke liye.

### 🤖 AI Prompt for On-Page SEO
Neeche diye gaye prompt (command) ko AI mein paste karein. Sirf Brackets `[ ]` waali jagah par apni details daal dein.

```text
I am writing a new webpage/blog post. 
Here is my Primary Keyword: [YAHAN APNA MAIN KEYWORD DALEIN]
Here are my Secondary/LSI Keywords: [YAHAN 3-4 RELATED KEYWORDS DALEIN]
Here is the rough draft, topic, or main points of my content: [YAHAN APNA ROUGH TOPIC YA DRAFT DALEIN]

Return each section with a clear label. Keep the tone human, not robotic.

Please generate a complete, highly optimized On-Page SEO strategy and content for this page. Provide the output in the following structure:

1. URL Slug: Provide a short, SEO-friendly URL slug.
2. Meta Title: Write a catchy, click-worthy Meta Title (maximum 60 characters) that includes the Primary Keyword.
3. Meta Description: Write an engaging Meta Description (maximum 160 characters) with a clear Call to Action (CTA), including the primary keyword.
4. Heading Structure: Provide an outline using proper H1, H2, and H3 tags. The H1 must be highly compelling.
5. Optimized Content: Write the full body content based on my draft/topic. 
    - Naturally integrate the Primary and LSI keywords. 
    - Ensure it is highly readable with short paragraphs and bullet points.
    - Write in an engaging, human tone that satisfies search intent.
6. Image Alt Text Suggestions: Provide 2-3 ideas for images I should include, along with proper SEO-optimized exact Alt Text for each.
```
