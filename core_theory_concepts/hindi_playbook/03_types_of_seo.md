# Types of SEO (SEO Ke 3 Main Pillars)

Kisi bhi website ko Google par rank karwane ke liye hume mainly 3 alag-alag areas mein kaam karna padta hai. Inhe SEO ke Types ya "Pillars" kehte hain:

## 1. On-Page SEO (Website ke andar ka kaam)
Yeh wo sab cheezein hain jo hum **apni website ke pages ke andar** karte hain. Is par hamara poora control hota hai.
*   **Keywords:** Wo words use karna jo log actually Google par search kar rahe hain.
*   **High-Quality Content:** Aisa content likhna jo user ki genuinely help kare.
*   **Titles & Headings:** Sahi se Title (H1) aur headings (H2, H3) ka use karna.
*   **Images:** Images mein 'Alt text' lagana taki search engine samajh sake ki photo kis baare mein hai.

## 2. Off-Page SEO (Website ke bahar ka kaam)
Yeh wo actions hain jo hum **apni website ke bahar (dusri websites par)** karte hain, yeh dikhane ke liye ki hamari site popular aur trustworthy hai.
*   **Backlinks:** Dusri popular websites se apni website ka link lena. (Yeh internet par 'vote' milne jaisa hota hai).
*   **Social Media:** Apne content ko Facebook, Twitter, ya LinkedIn par share karke traffic lana.
*   **Brand Mentions:** Jab dusre log ya blogs apni website par aapke brand ki baat karte hain.

## 3. Technical SEO (Website ke piche ka system)
Iska seedha relation content se nahi hota. Ye website ke **technical structure** ko theek karta hai taki search engine bots aasaani se site ko padh (crawl/index) sakein.
*   **Website Speed:** Site ko itna optimize karna ki wo bahot fast load ho.
*   **Mobile-Friendly:** Website mobile phones par bhi perfectly chalni chahiye aur achi dikhni chahiye.
*   **XML Sitemaps:** Search engines ko ek "map" dena jisse wo site ke baki pages easily dhoondh lein.
*   **Security:** SSL certificate (HTTPS) lagana taki website safe aur secure rahe.
