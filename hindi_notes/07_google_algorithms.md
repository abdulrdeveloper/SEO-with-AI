# Google Algorithms: Major aur Minor Updates

---

## 1. Google Algorithm Kya Hai?
Google Algorithm un rules, formulas, aur AI systems ka ek set hai jo yeh decide karta hai ki Google search mein kaunsi website 1st number par aayegi aur kaunsi hategi.
Google apne in rules ko lagatar update karta rehta hai taaki spam (kachra) websites ko hataya ja sake aur achi websites ko upar laya ja sake.

---

## 2. Major Google Algorithms
Yeh woh sabse bade updates hain jinhone SEO ki duniya ko poori tarah badal kar rakh diya. (Image mein 8 diye gaye the, humne 2 naye AI updates add karke list ko 10 kar diya hai):

1.  **Panda** — Iska focus content quality par tha. Isne copy-paste aur bekaar content wali sites ko dande maare (penalize kiya).
2.  **Penguin** — Iska focus Backlinks par tha. Jisne fake ya kharide hue links banaye the, uski site rank se hata di gayi.
3.  **Hummingbird** — Isne keywords ke bajaye user ke "Intent" (woh kya dhoondhna chahta hai) par focus kiya.
4.  **Pigeon** — Yeh Local SEO aur Google maps ki ranking sudharne ke liye laya gaya tha.
5.  **Mobile (Mobilegeddon)** — Jinki website mobile par dhang se nahi khulti thi, unki ranking gira di gayi.
6.  **RankBrain** — Yeh Google ka pehla AI/Machine Learning system tha jo naye aur ajeeb searches ka matlab samajhta tha.
7.  **Medic** — Isne health, finance aur medical (YMYL) websites par asar dala, jinke paas trust aur authority nahi thi unhe uda diya.
8.  **Core Updates** — Yeh Google ke regular bade updates hain jo saal mein 2-3 baar aate hain overall search ko behtar saaf karne ke liye.
9.  **BERT** *(Bonus)* — AI language update jisne Google ko insaano wali bhasha aur context samajhna sikhaya.
10. **Helpful Content Update** *(Bonus)* — Jo log sirf bots ke liye likhte the (ya AI generated spam phelate the) unhe rank se nikal bahar kiya.

## 3. Minor Google Algorithms
Yeh chhote updates the jo kisi specific type ke spam ya problem ko theek karne ke liye aaye the:

1.  **Florida** — Yeh sabse pehle updates mein se tha jisne keyword-stuffing (ek word bar-bar likhna) ko roka.
2.  **Big Daddy** — Isne Google ke index karne ke tareeqe aur URL redirects ko behter banaya.
3.  **EMD (Exact Match Domain)** — Jo log sirf keyword wala domain le lete the (`best-laptop.com`) par andar content raddi hota tha, unhe penalize kiya.
4.  **Jagger** — Isne unnatural link building aur link-exchange scams ko target kiya.
5.  **Payday** — Bahut zyada spammy topics (jaise loans, casino) wali sites ko saaf karne ke liye The.
6.  **Vince** — Isne badi aur trusted "Brands" ko thoda extra ranking boost diya.
7.  **Caffeine** — Yeh algorithm nahi, balki Google ka ek indexing system upgrade tha jisne indexing speed ko bohot fast kar diya.
8.  **Quality Updates** — Bina bataye aane waale chhote-chhote updates jinse content quality check hoti thi.
9.  **Fred** — Un websites ko thoka jo content kam aur ads/affiliate links zyada dikhate the.
10. **Page Layout Algorithm** — Agar aapki site open hote hi sirf ads hi ads dikhein (content ke liye scroll karna pade), toh ye update aisi sites ko penalty deta tha.
