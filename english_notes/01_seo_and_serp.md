# What is SEO & SERP? (Simple Notes for Students)

## 1. What is SEO? (Search Engine Optimization)
In simple words, SEO is the process of improving your website so it shows up at the top of Google search results. 
If I have a blog, and I want people to find it when they search for a related topic, I need to use SEO.

*   **Basically:** Optimizing your site according to Google's rules to get free (organic) traffic without paying for ads.
*   **Why is it important?:** Because nobody goes to the second page of Google! Everyone clicks on the first few results on page one.

## 2. What is SERP? (Search Engine Results Page)
SERP is the page that shows up AFTER you search for something on Google.
For example, if you search "Best laptops", the whole page filled with website links, images, and videos is called the SERP.

*   **Simple meaning:** The search engine's result page where we want our website to show up.
